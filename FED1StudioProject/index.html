History of Kansas City Music Company

Established in 1922, the Kansas City Music Company has been helping young and old musician's alike discover or rediscover their love of music for nearly 100 years. Whether you are just beginning your music journey with a learner's instrument, an enthusiast who want's to brush up on their skills, or an advanced learner playing at the professional level, we have a range of dedicated teacher's and staff ready to help you learn and grow as a musician.
